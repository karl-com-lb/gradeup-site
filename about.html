About Grade Up

A platform built by students, for students — modernizing tutoring in Lebanon.

Our Story

Grade Up was created by students who experienced how difficult it is to find the right tutor. We built a platform where learning becomes simpler, more relatable, and more effective.

Our Mission

To make high-quality education accessible and connect students with tutors who truly understand them.
